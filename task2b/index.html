Top 6 movies of the year 2023

"Avatar: The Way of Water" is a highly anticipated sequel to the 2009 film "Avatar" directed by James Cameron, and follows the new adventures of the Na'vi on the planet Pandora.

Avatar: The Way of Water

"Black Panther: Wakanda Forever" is the highly anticipated sequel to the 2018 Marvel film "Black Panther," which pays homage to the late Chadwick Boseman and explores themes of colonialism and power struggles in Wakanda.

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever

"The Batman" is a highly anticipated superhero film directed by Matt Reeves and starring Robert Pattinson in the titular role, exploring the darker and more complex aspects of the character's persona.

The Batman

"Jurassic World: Dominion" is the highly anticipated third installment in the Jurassic World film series which showcases the final battle between dinosaurs and humans.

Jurassic World: Dominion

"Mission: Impossible 7" is the highly anticipated action film directed by Christopher McQuarrie, starring Tom Cruise as Ethan Hunt, which explores the world of international espionage and thrilling stunts.

Mission: Impossible 7

"Fantastic Beasts 3: The Secrets of Dumbledore" is the upcoming third installment in the Fantastic Beasts film series, directed by David Yates and starring Eddie Redmayne, which follows the adventures of Newt Scamander and explores the wizarding world of J.K. Rowling's imagination.

Fantastic Beasts 3
