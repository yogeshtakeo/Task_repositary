Nepal Tourism

Nepal is a small, landlocked country located in South Asia. It is home to over 29 million people and has a diverse population made up of over 125 different ethnic groups. The official language of Nepal is Nepali, and the country's currency is the Nepalese rupee.

Nepal is known for its stunning natural beauty, with majestic mountain ranges, lush green forests, and beautiful rivers. The country is home to several of the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest, which stands at 8,848 meters above sea level. Trekking and mountaineering are popular activities in Nepal, with visitors coming from all over the world to climb its mountains and explore its breathtaking landscapes.

Aside from its natural beauty, Nepal is also known for its rich cultural heritage. The country has a long and fascinating history, with evidence of human settlement dating back to the Neolithic period. Nepal has several tourism destinations that attract millions of visitors every year.

Mount Everest

Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world, with an elevation of 8,848 meters (29,029 feet) above sea level. It is located on the border between Nepal and Tibet (China) and is one of the most iconic and sought-after destinations for mountaineers and adventure seekers from around the world.

Kathmandu Durbar Square

Kathmandu Durbar Square is a historic site located in the heart of Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal. It is a complex of ancient temples, palaces, and courtyards that were once the royal residence of the Malla and Shah kings of Nepal. The square is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Nepal.

Pokhara

Pokhara is a scenic city located in the Kaski district of Nepal, about 200 kilometers west of Kathmandu. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including its tranquil lakes, soaring mountains, and breathtaking waterfalls. Pokhara is a popular tourist destination in Nepal and is often referred to as the "Gateway to the Himalayas."

Chitwan National Park

Chitwan National Park is another popular tourist destination in Nepal. It is located in the southern part of the country and is known for its diverse wildlife, including tigers, rhinos, and elephants. Visitors can enjoy jungle safaris, bird watching, and cultural tours in the park.
