Holographic technology

Holographic technology is a method of creating three-dimensional images using laser light. It is commonly used in security features, such as holographic stickers on credit cards, and in entertainment, such as holographic displays at concerts or in movies. Wrap-around glasses are a style of eyewear that features a frame that fully surrounds the lenses, providing additional protection from sunlight and other environmental factors. They are commonly used for outdoor activities such as sports or driving.
