Overland Park, KS

My name is Sabin Sapkota. I am from Overland Park, KS. Overland Park is a city in Kansas known for its many parks, top-rated schools, and diverse communities. Some of the top parks are mentioned below:

  • Roe Park: A popular park for families with a playground, tennis courts, picnic areas, and a fitness trail.
  • South Lake Park: A beautiful park with a scenic lake, walking trail, and fishing dock.
  • Black Hoof Park: A large park with hiking trails, a fishing lake, a nature center, and a beach area.
